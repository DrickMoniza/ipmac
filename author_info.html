PMMA-CME-IPMAC

This course covers the knowledge, understanding and proficiency of the basic control engineering;
the operation, maintenance and repair of electrical, electronic and control systems. The competencies
covered in this course is in accordance with STCW ’78 code as amended. It aims to provide students
with necessary knowledge, understanding and proficiency listed under column 2 of table A-III/1 and A-III/2.
